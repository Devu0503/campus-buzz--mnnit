CHITRASANGAM

Behold yourselves as the excitement for Chitrasangam blooms. Witness one of the most anticipated events hosted by The Media House of MNNIT. This year, we are excited to bring you a wide range of competitions that highlight various forms of media; ‘Chitravali’, which challenges participants to capture the essence of a moment through photography, ‘Chitrana’, which encourages participants to express their creativity through visual storytelling, and ‘Chalchitra’, which invites participants to showcase their skills in film-making. Event Date: 17th March to 20th March Come and be a part of this incredible journey of self-expression, exploration, and discovery
