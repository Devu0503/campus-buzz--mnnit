Culrav '23.

Culrav is the annual cultural festival of Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT) located in Allahabad (Prayagraj), Uttar Pradesh, India. The festival is usually held in the month of March or April and lasts for three days. Culrav is a popular event that attracts participants from various colleges and universities across India. The festival features a variety of cultural events, including dance, music, drama, and literary activities. It also hosts several competitions, workshops, and exhibitions related to art, literature, and technology. Apart from the cultural events, Culrav also features various fun activities like gaming zones, food stalls, and adventure sports. It provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and skills, and also promotes cultural exchange and understanding among students from different parts of the country.
